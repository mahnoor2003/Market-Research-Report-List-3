<p><ul><li>Convex Array</li><li>Concave Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Achromatic lens elements are commonly found in two market types: convex array and concave array. In the convex array market, these lens elements are used to correct chromatic aberration by combining two materials with different dispersion properties. Conversely, in the concave array market, achromatic lens elements are designed to disperse light and correct for spherical aberration. Both types of markets rely on the unique properties of achromatic lens elements to improve optical performance and image quality in various applications.</p></p>
