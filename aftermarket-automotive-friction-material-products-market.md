<p><p>Aftermarket automotive friction material products refer to replacement parts for vehicles that provide friction to help stop or control the movement of the vehicle. These products are categorized into three main types: brake linings, brake pads, and clutch facings. Brake linings are used in drum brakes to create friction, while brake pads are used in disc brakes. Clutch facings are used in the clutch system to engage and disengage the engine from the transmission. Each type of friction material product plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety and performance of the vehicle.</p></p>
