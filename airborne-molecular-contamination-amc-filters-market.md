<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Airborne Molecular Contamination (AMC) Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemisorption Filters (Activated Carbon or Aluminas)</li><li>Bonded Media Panels (Activated Carbon Formed into Monolithic (Single-Piece) Panels)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airborne Molecular Contamination (AMC) Filters Market includes two main types: Chemisorption Filters, which are made of activated carbon or aluminas that chemically bond with contaminants, and Bonded Media Panels, which consist of activated carbon formed into monolithic (single-piece) panels for efficient filtration. Chemisorption filters are effective in removing a wide range of contaminants, while bonded media panels provide a large surface area for adsorption, making them ideal for high-contamination environments. These filters play a crucial role in maintaining clean air quality in various industries.</p></p>
