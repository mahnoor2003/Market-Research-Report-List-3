<p><strong>The Aircraft Battery Management System (BMS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized</li><li>Modular</li><li>Distributed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft Battery Management System (BMS) market can be categorized into three types based on their architecture: Centralized, Modular, and Distributed. </p><p>Centralized BMS involves a single control unit managing the entire battery system. Modular BMS consists of multiple identical modules working together to manage the battery system. Distributed BMS includes several smaller control units distributed throughout the battery system to manage different sections independently. Each type has its advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements and preferences of the aircraft manufacturer.</p></p>
