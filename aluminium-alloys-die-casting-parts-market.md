<p><p>Aluminium Alloys Die Casting Parts Market includes various types such as Vacuum Die Casting and Pressure Die Casting. In Vacuum Die Casting, the process involves reducing air pressure inside the die cavity before injecting the molten metal, resulting in less porosity and better surface finish. On the other hand, Pressure Die Casting uses high pressure to force the molten metal into the die cavity, producing complex shapes with high dimensional accuracy. Both methods are widely used in industries for manufacturing aluminium alloy parts.</p></p>
