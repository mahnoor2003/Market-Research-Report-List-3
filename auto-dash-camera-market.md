<p><p>Single channel auto dash cameras are designed to record footage from a single perspective, typically facing forward. These cameras are ideal for capturing incidents that occur in front of the vehicle. On the other hand, multi-channel auto dash cameras can record footage from multiple angles simultaneously, providing a more comprehensive view of the surroundings. This type of camera is commonly used by commercial vehicles or fleet operators to monitor the inside and outside of the vehicle. Both types of cameras serve the purpose of providing visual evidence in case of accidents or disputes on the road.</p></p>
