<p><p>Auto-injectors market can be categorized into two types: disposable auto-injectors and reusable auto-injectors. Disposable auto-injectors are designed for one-time use and are disposed of after a single injection. On the other hand, reusable auto-injectors can be refilled and used multiple times for injections. Both types offer convenience and ease of use for patients who need to self-administer medication. The choice between disposable and reusable auto-injectors depends on factors such as cost, sustainability, and frequency of medication administration.</p></p>
