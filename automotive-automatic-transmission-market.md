<p><p>Automotive automatic transmission systems come in various types such as Automated Manual Transmission (AMT), Automatic Transmission (AT), Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT), and Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT). AMT is a manual transmission that can automatically shift gears, AT uses a torque converter to shift gears automatically, DCT uses two separate clutches for quicker gear shifts, and CVT provides a smooth and continuous range of gear ratios. Each type offers different driving experiences and benefits to cater to various preferences and needs of drivers.</p></p>
