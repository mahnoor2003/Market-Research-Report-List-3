<p><p>The Automotive Conventional Powertrain IC market consists of Engine Management ICs, Alternator Regulators, and Constant Current Control ICs. Engine Management ICs are responsible for controlling various functions in the engine, such as fuel injection and ignition timing. Alternator Regulators help regulate the voltage output of the alternator to ensure proper charging of the vehicle's battery. Constant Current Control ICs are used to regulate the flow of current in various components of the vehicle, ensuring efficient and safe operation of the powertrain system.</p></p>
