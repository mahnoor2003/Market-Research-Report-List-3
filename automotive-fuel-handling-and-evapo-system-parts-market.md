<p><p>The Automotive Fuel Handling and Evapo System Parts market includes various components such as EVAP canisters, vent control valves, purge valves/sensors, liquid-vapor separators, and other related parts. The EVAP canister is responsible for storing fuel vapors and preventing them from escaping into the environment. The vent control valve regulates the flow of vapor between the fuel tank and the EVAP canister. The purge valve/sensor controls the release of stored fuel vapors into the engine for combustion. Liquid-vapor separators help separate liquid fuel from vapor before entering the engine. Other parts in this market play key roles in the overall fuel handling and evapo system operation.</p></p>
