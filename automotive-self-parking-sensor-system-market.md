<p><p>Automotive self-parking sensor systems are available in various types, including ultrasonic sensor system, radar sensor system, image sensor system, and others. Ultrasonic sensor systems use sound waves to detect objects and assist in parking maneuvers. Radar sensor systems use radio waves to detect obstacles and provide accurate parking guidance. Image sensor systems use cameras to capture visual information and aid in parking. Other types of sensor systems may include infrared sensors or laser sensors, each offering unique capabilities for convenient and safe parking assistance.</p></p>
