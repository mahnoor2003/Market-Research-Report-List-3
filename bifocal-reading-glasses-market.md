<p><p>Bifocal reading glasses come in various types, including Flap-Top Bifocals and Round Bifocals. Flap-Top Bifocals have a distinct line dividing the lens into two sections, with the top portion for distance vision and the bottom for close-up reading. This design allows the wearer to easily switch between the two prescriptions by simply flipping the lens up or down. Round Bifocals, on the other hand, have a round segment at the bottom of the lens for reading, making them a fashionable and functional choice for those who need both distance and close-up vision correction.</p></p>
