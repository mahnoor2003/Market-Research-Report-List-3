Connectors with a pitch size between 1.00 mm to 2.00 mm are considered standard pitch connectors and are used in a wide range of electronic applications. Connectors with a pitch size greater than 2.00 mm are known as wide pitch connectors and are used in applications where robustness and reliability are key factors.</p></p>
