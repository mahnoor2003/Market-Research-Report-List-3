<p><strong>The Capacitor Banks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LV & MV Capacitor Banks</li><li>HV Capacitor Banks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitor banks are categorized based on the voltage levels they operate at. LV (Low Voltage) capacitor banks are designed for voltages below 1 kV, typically used in residential and commercial applications. MV (Medium Voltage) capacitor banks are used in voltages ranging from 1 kV to 69 kV, suitable for industrial and utility applications. On the other hand, HV (High Voltage) capacitor banks are designed for voltages above 69 kV, used in high-power transmission and distribution systems. Each type serves specific voltage requirements and applications in the electrical grid.</p></p>
