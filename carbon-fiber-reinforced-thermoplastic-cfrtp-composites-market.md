<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (CFRTP) composites are used in various industries like automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods due to their high strength-to-weight ratio. There are two main types of CFRTP composites: Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based and Pitch-based. PAN-based CFRTP composites are widely used due to their superior mechanical properties and easier processing. On the other hand, Pitch-based CFRTP composites offer higher thermal conductivity and are preferred in applications where high-temperature resistance is required. Both types have their unique advantages and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
