<p><p>Carbon fiber resin market is divided into two main types based on the precursor material used to make the carbon fiber. The PAN-based carbon fiber is produced from polyacrylonitrile and is the most common type in the market due to its high performance and versatility. On the other hand, pitch-based carbon fiber is produced from a precursor material called pitch and is known for its high strength and modulus. Both types of carbon fiber have unique properties and applications in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment.</p></p>
