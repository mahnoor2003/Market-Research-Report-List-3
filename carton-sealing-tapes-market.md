<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive</li><li>Water-activated</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carton sealing tapes are primarily categorized into three types: pressure sensitive, water-activated, and other. Pressure sensitive tapes are easy to use, simply requiring pressure to adhere to surfaces. Water-activated tapes require moisture to activate their adhesive properties, providing strong and secure sealing. The "other" category includes specialty tapes that cater to specific needs, such as temperature resistance or tamper-evident features. Each type offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications, catering to a wide range of sealing requirements in various industries.</p></p>
