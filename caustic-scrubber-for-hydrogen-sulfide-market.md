<p><p>Caustic scrubbers for hydrogen sulfide removal come in two main types - vertical scrubbers (also known as counter-flow scrubbers) and horizontal scrubbers (also known as cross-flow scrubbers). Vertical scrubbers are designed to allow the gas and liquid to flow in opposite directions, maximizing contact between the two for efficient absorption of hydrogen sulfide. Horizontal scrubbers, on the other hand, allow the gas and liquid to flow perpendicular to each other, promoting a more uniform distribution of the gas throughout the scrubbing solution.</p></p>
