<p><p>Cervical artificial discs are medical devices used to replace damaged or degenerated discs in the neck. These discs come in various types, including metal and biopolymer. Metal artificial discs are made of materials like cobalt-chromium alloys or titanium, providing durability and stability. Biopolymer discs, on the other hand, are made of materials that mimic the properties of natural tissue, offering a more flexible and natural feel. The choice between metal and biopolymer artificial discs depends on factors such as patient preferences and medical requirements.</p></p>
