<p><p>Chilled beam systems are a type of HVAC technology that uses convection and radiant cooling to control the temperature of a space. There are three main types of chilled beam systems in the market: active chilled beams, passive chilled beams, and multiservice chilled beams. Active chilled beams use a separate air supply for ventilation, passive chilled beams rely on natural convection for air circulation, and multiservice chilled beams incorporate additional features such as lighting and sprinklers. Each type offers different benefits and suitability for various applications.</p></p>
