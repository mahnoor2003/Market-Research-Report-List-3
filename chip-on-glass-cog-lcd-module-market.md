<p><p>A Chip-on-Glass (COG) LCD Module is a type of display module where the driver IC is directly mounted on the glass substrate of the LCD panel, eliminating the need for a separate PCB. This design helps in reducing the overall size and thickness of the display, making it suitable for applications where space is limited, such as smartphones, automotive displays, and wearable devices.</p><p>The Chip-on-Glass (COG) LCD Module Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period.
