<p><p>CMOS Analog Switches are electronic components used to control the flow of analog signals in a circuit. They come in various configurations, with SPST (Single Pole Single Throw) and SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) being common types. SPST switches have one input and one output, making them suitable for simple on/off applications. SPDT switches have one input and two outputs, allowing for more complex signal routing. Both types find applications in various industries like telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
