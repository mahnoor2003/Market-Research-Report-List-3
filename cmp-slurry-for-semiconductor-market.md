<p><ul><li>Alumina Slurry</li><li>Colloidal Silica Slurry</li><li>Ceria Slurry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP slurry for semiconductor market includes various types such as Alumina Slurry, Colloidal Silica Slurry, and Ceria Slurry. Alumina Slurry is made of aluminum oxide particles used for polishing and planarization. Colloidal Silica Slurry consists of ultrafine silica particles suspended in a liquid, ideal for oxide and metal polishing. Ceria Slurry contains cerium oxide particles used for chemical mechanical polishing processes. These different types of slurries cater to specific requirements in the semiconductor industry for achieving precise and efficient polishing results.</p></p>
