<p><strong>The Color-shifting Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Coating</li><li>Powder Coating</li><li>Water-based Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Color-shifting coatings are available in three main types: solvent-based coatings, powder coatings, and water-based coatings. Solvent-based coatings contain liquefied substances that evaporate during the curing process, leaving a hard, protective film. Powder coatings are composed of finely ground particles that are electrostatically charged and applied to the substrate before being cured in an oven. Water-based coatings use water as the primary solvent, making them more environmentally friendly. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in various industries based on specific requirements.</p></p>
