<p><p>Commercial control dampers are classified into two main types: light duty and heavy duty. Light duty dampers are typically used in residential and small commercial applications where airflow control is less critical. On the other hand, heavy duty dampers are commonly found in larger commercial and industrial settings where precise airflow control is vital. Heavy duty dampers are designed to withstand high air velocities and pressure differentials, making them suitable for demanding applications. Both types of dampers play a crucial role in HVAC systems by regulating airflow and maintaining desired indoor conditions.</p></p>
