<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Humidifiers</li><li>Water Spray Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Humidifier Market includes two main types: Vapor Type Humidifiers and Water Spray Humidifiers. Vapor Type Humidifiers generate moisture by heating water to create steam, while Water Spray Humidifiers emit droplets of water into the air to increase humidity levels. Vapor Type Humidifiers are typically more efficient at raising humidity levels quickly in large commercial spaces, while Water Spray Humidifiers are often used in smaller areas or where a more subtle increase in humidity is desired.</p></p>
