<p><p>Commercial patio umbrellas come in three main types: </p><p>1. Wall Mounted Umbrellas: These are attached to a wall or structure and provide shade without taking up floor space, making them ideal for smaller outdoor areas.</p><p>2. Centre Pole Umbrellas: These umbrellas are supported by a central pole and are commonly used in outdoor dining areas or poolside settings.</p><p>3. Offset Pole Umbrellas: Also known as cantilever umbrellas, these are supported by an offset pole, allowing for maximum shade coverage without the obstruction of a center pole. They are often used in larger outdoor spaces such as hotel pools or restaurants.</p></p>
