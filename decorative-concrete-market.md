<p><p>Decorative concrete is a versatile building material that can be used in various types of applications. Exposed concrete involves removing the top layer to reveal the aggregates, creating a textured finish. Stamped concrete is imprinted with patterns and textures to resemble other materials such as brick or stone. Polished concrete has a sleek, glossy finish achieved through grinding and polishing. Engraved concrete features designs and patterns carved into the surface. Stained concrete involves adding color for a vibrant, customized look. Other types include overlays, countertops, and vertical treatments.</p></p>
