<p><p>The dental filling materials market can be categorized into several types, including silver amalgam, composite resin, glass ionomer cements, and others. Silver amalgam fillings are a durable and cost-effective option but can be more visible in the mouth. Composite resin fillings are tooth-colored and blend in with natural teeth, making them a popular choice for aesthetic reasons. Glass ionomer cements are a fluoride-releasing option that can help prevent further decay. Other types of fillings include gold and ceramic materials.</p></p>
