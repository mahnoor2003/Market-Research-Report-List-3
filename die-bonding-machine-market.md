<p><p>Die bonding machines are used in the semiconductor industry for attaching delicate electronic components to substrates. They come in three main types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic machines require little to no human intervention, maximizing efficiency and precision. Semi-automatic machines have some automated functions but also require human input. Manual machines are operated entirely by hand, offering greater control but slower speeds. Each type caters to different production needs and budgets within the die bonding machine market.</p></p>
