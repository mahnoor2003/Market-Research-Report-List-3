<p><p>Dimming film is available in two main types - self adhesive and non adhesive. Self adhesive dimming film has a sticky backing that allows for easy installation onto various surfaces such as windows or glass doors. Non adhesive dimming film, on the other hand, does not have a sticky backing and is typically used for temporary applications or when a more temporary solution is needed. Both types of dimming film offer the ability to control the amount of light entering a space, providing privacy and glare reduction.</p></p>
