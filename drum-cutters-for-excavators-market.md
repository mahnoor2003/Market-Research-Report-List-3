<p><p>Drum cutters for excavators come in two main types: transverse and axial. Transverse drum cutters are designed to cut through materials in a perpendicular direction, while axial drum cutters work in a parallel orientation. This allows for greater versatility in applications, as transverse cutters are typically used for grinding and cutting tasks, while axial cutters are more commonly used for excavating and trenching. Both types offer efficient and precise cutting capabilities, making them valuable tools in construction and demolition projects.</p></p>
