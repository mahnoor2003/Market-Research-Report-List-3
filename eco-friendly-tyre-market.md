<p><p>Eco-friendly tire market includes two main types: All-Steel and Semi-Steel tires. All-Steel tires are made with steel belts running around the entire circumference of the tire, providing excellent durability and stability. Semi-Steel tires have steel belts running only across the tread area, offering a balance between performance and cost-effectiveness. Both types of tires are designed to reduce environmental impact by using sustainable materials and producing lower levels of emissions during manufacturing and use. These eco-friendly tires cater to consumers looking for sustainable and reliable tire options.</p></p>
