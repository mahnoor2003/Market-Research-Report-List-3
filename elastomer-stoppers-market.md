<p><p>Elastomer stoppers are commonly used in laboratories and pharmaceutical industries for sealing containers. There are two main types of elastomer stoppers: solid elastomer stoppers and hollow elastomer stoppers. Solid elastomer stoppers are made of a single piece of elastomer material and provide a tight seal when inserted into a container. On the other hand, hollow elastomer stoppers have a hollow core that allows for easier insertion and removal from containers. Both types of elastomer stoppers are essential for maintaining the integrity of samples and chemicals stored in containers.</p></p>
