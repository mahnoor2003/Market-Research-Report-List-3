<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Wireless Charger market offers two main types of technologies - Magnetic Resonance Technology and Inductive Charging Technology. Magnetic Resonance Technology uses magnetic fields to transfer energy between the charging pad and the vehicle, allowing for greater flexibility in positioning. Inductive Charging Technology, on the other hand, utilizes magnetic fields to transfer energy through coils in the charging pad and the vehicle. Both technologies offer efficient and convenient ways to charge electric vehicles without the need for physical plugs and cords.</p></p>
