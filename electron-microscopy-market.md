<p><p>The Electron Microscopy Market comprises several types of microscopes, including Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM) and Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM). TEM uses a beam of electrons to create images of extremely small objects in high resolution, while SEM scans a sample with a focused beam of electrons to produce detailed surface images. Other types of electron microscopes include Cryo-electron microscopes, Reflection Electron Microscopes, and Environmental Scanning Electron Microscopes, each serving specific purposes in various fields such as material science, nanotechnology, biology, and medicine.</p></p>
