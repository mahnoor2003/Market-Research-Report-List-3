<p><p>Endoscopic Mucosal Resection (EMR) Devices are used for the removal of abnormal tissue from the gastrointestinal tract. There are three main types of devices used in EMR procedures: Entry Needles, which are used to inject fluid into the tissue to create a lift; Mucosectomy Snares, which are loop-like devices used to remove the lifted tissue; and Probes, which are used to accurately locate and target the abnormal tissue for removal. These devices play a crucial role in the successful and safe execution of EMR procedures.</p></p>
