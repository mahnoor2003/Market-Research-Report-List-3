The market types for EVA foam include Extrusion Molding, Injection Foaming Molding, and Compression Molding. Extrusion Molding involves forcing melted EVA material through a die to create a specific shape. Injection Foaming Molding injects the material into a mold cavity under high pressure to produce complex shapes. Compression Molding uses heat and pressure to mold EVA foam into the desired shape.</p></p>
