<p><strong>The Fiber Laser Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DFB Laser Chip</li><li>VCSEL Chip</li><li>EML Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Laser Chip market is categorized into three types: Distributed Feedback (DFB) Laser Chip, Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Laser (VCSEL) Chip, and Electro-absorption Modulated Laser (EML) Chip. DFB Laser Chip offers high power and narrow linewidth for precise applications. VCSEL Chip provides efficient heat dissipation and low-cost production for short-range communication. EML Chip combines direct modulation and high-speed performance for long-distance transmission. Each type caters to specific needs in fiber laser technology, offering a range of capabilities for different applications.</p></p>
