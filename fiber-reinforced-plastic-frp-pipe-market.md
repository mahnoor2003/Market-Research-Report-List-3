<p><p>Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP) Pipe Market consists of three main types: Glass-Reinforced Plastic (GRP), Glass-Reinforced Epoxy (GRE), and Glass-Reinforced Vinylester (GRV). GRP pipes are made with a thermosetting resin and glass fiber reinforcement, offering high corrosion resistance. GRE pipes are produced using an epoxy resin, providing excellent strength and chemical resistance. GRV pipes are manufactured with a vinylester resin, offering superior resistance to harsh chemicals. Each type of FRP pipe caters to different applications based on their unique properties.</p></p>
