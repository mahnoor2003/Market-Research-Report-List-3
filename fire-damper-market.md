<p><p>Fire damper market types include mechanical dampers, intumescent dampers, and air transfer fire dampers. Mechanical dampers are traditional dampers that close when triggered by heat or smoke. Intumescent dampers are designed to expand when exposed to high temperatures, sealing off the area and preventing the spread of fire. Air transfer fire dampers are used in HVAC systems to prevent the spread of smoke and fire between compartments. Each type of damper serves a specific purpose in fire safety and prevention.</p></p>
