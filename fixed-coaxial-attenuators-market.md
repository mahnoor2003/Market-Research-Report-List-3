<p><p>Fixed coaxial attenuators are passive devices used to reduce the amplitude of an electrical signal without affecting its waveform. They are commonly used in RF and microwave systems to control signal power levels and minimize signal reflection. These attenuators have fixed attenuation levels that are specified in decibels (dB) and are available in various connector types such as SMA, N-Type, and TNC.</p><p>The Fixed Coaxial Attenuators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period. The rising adoption of IoT devices, aerospace and defense applications, and telecommunication networks is also contributing to the market's expansion.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in RF and microwave technologies, such as the development of high-frequency fixed coaxial attenuators with higher power-handling capabilities and lower insertion loss, are fueling market growth.
