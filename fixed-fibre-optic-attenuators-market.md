<p><p>Fixed Fibre Optic Attenuators are used in fibre optic communication systems to reduce the power of the optical signal. They come in different types like Bulkhead, Plug, Panel Mount, and In-Line Jumper. Bulkhead attenuators are mounted at the bulkhead of a connector panel, plug attenuators are directly connected to the connector, and panel mount attenuators are attached to a panel. In-Line Jumpers are used between two connectors to reduce the signal power. These types cater to different installation needs and are essential components in fibre optic networks.</p></p>
