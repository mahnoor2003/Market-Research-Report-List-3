<p><p>Flat glass tempering machines are classified into two main types based on their orientation: horizontal type and vertical type. Horizontal type machines are designed to process flat glass sheets in a horizontal position, which allows for high production capacities and efficient heating and cooling processes. On the other hand, vertical type machines process glass in a vertical position, offering flexibility in handling different glass sizes and shapes. Both types of machines cater to the unique needs of glass manufacturers in terms of production volume and customization requirements.</p></p>
