<p><strong>The Flexible Packaging Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Hot Melt</li><li>Reactive</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible Packaging Adhesives market can be categorized into five types based on their composition and method of application. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly and easy to use. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding but may emit harmful fumes. Hot melt adhesives provide fast bonding and high flexibility. Reactive adhesives require a curing process for bonding. Other types of adhesives include pressure-sensitive adhesives, UV-curable adhesives, and acrylic adhesives. Each type of adhesive offers unique properties and is suited for specific packaging applications.</p></p>
