<p><p>Free radical light curing resin market consists of four main types: oligomers, monomers, photoinitiators, and additives. Oligomers are prepolymers that provide viscosity and mechanical properties. Monomers act as reactive diluents to reduce viscosity and improve flowability. Photoinitiators are compounds that initiate the curing process when exposed to light. Additives are used to enhance specific properties such as adhesion, flexibility, or color stability. Each type plays a crucial role in the functionality and performance of light-curing resins in various applications.</p></p>
