<p><p>FRP panels are used in various industries and applications, with different types of composites available in the market. Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer (GFRP) composites are known for their versatility and cost-effectiveness. Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer (CFRP) composites offer high strength-to-weight ratio and are commonly used in aerospace and automotive industries. Basalt Fiber Reinforced Polymer (BFRP) composites are known for their high thermal and chemical resistance. Aramid Fiber Reinforced Polymer (AFRP) composites provide excellent impact resistance and durability, making them suitable for ballistic protection applications.</p></p>
