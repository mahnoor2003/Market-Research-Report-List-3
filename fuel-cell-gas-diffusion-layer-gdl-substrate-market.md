<p><p>Fuel cell gas diffusion layer (GDL) substrates come in two main types: carbon fiber paper substrate and carbon cloth substrate. Carbon fiber paper substrate is made of thin layers of carbon fibers bonded together, providing high conductivity and porosity. On the other hand, carbon cloth substrate consists of woven carbon fibers, offering flexibility and durability. Both substrates play a crucial role in facilitating the diffusion of gases within fuel cells, ultimately enhancing their efficiency and performance.</p></p>
