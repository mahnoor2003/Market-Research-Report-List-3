<p><p>The Gasoline In-Cylinder Direct Injection (GDI) System market includes various types of components such as fuel injectors, engine control equipment, sensors, fuel rails, and fuel pumps. Fuel injectors are responsible for delivering gasoline directly into the combustion chamber, while engine control equipment regulates the injection process. Sensors monitor various parameters to optimize engine performance. Fuel rails distribute fuel to the injectors, while fuel pumps ensure a steady flow of gasoline. Together, these components form the GDI System essential for efficient combustion in modern engines.</p></p>
