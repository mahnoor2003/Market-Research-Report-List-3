Plant-based gluten-free protein powders, on the other hand, are made from sources such as peas, rice, or hemp, making them suitable for individuals looking for a non-animal protein source. Both options cater to those with gluten sensitivities or celiac disease, providing a safe and nutritious protein supplement.</p></p>
