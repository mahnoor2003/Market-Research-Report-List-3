<p><p>The GPS Track Tracker market can be categorized into different types based on their installation methods. Wiring Type trackers are typically hardwired into the vehicle's electrical system for a secure and permanent installation. OBD Interface Type trackers are plug-and-play devices that connect to the vehicle's OBD port for easy and quick installation. Strong Magnetic Free Installation trackers have powerful magnets that allow for covert and easy attachment to any metal surface on the vehicle without the need for wiring or a permanent installation.</p></p>
