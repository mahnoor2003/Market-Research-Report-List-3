<p><p>Graphene market can be divided into three main types: Graphene Powder, Graphene Oxide, and Graphene Film. Graphene Powder refers to the fine particles of graphene that are used in various industries for their high conductivity and strength. Graphene Oxide is a form of graphene with oxygen-containing groups that make it easier to disperse in solvents, making it suitable for applications like batteries and sensors. Graphene Film is a thin layer of graphene that is used in electronic devices for its excellent conductivity and transparency.</p></p>
