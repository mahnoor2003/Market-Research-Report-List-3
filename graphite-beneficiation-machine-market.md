<p><p>Graphite beneficiation machines are utilized in various processes such as flotation, electrical separation, and re-election to improve the quality and purity of graphite ore. </p><p>Flotation involves separating valuable minerals through their hydrophobicity, while electrical separation uses differences in conductivity to separate graphite from other minerals. Re-election method utilizes differences in density to separate graphite particles from impurities. </p><p>Each of these market types plays a crucial role in enhancing the value and usability of graphite ore for industrial applications.</p></p>
