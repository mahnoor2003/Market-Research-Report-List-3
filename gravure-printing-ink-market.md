<p><p>Gravure printing ink market is divided into three main types: Conventional Gravure Ink, Plastic Gravure Ink, and Alcohol Soluble Gravure Ink. Conventional Gravure Ink is widely used for printing on paper and cardboard materials. Plastic Gravure Ink is specifically formulated for printing on plastic substrates such as polyethylene and polyester. Each type of ink is tailored to meet the specific requirements of different printing applications.</p></p>
