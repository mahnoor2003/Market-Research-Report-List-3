<p><p>Guided wave radar liquid level sensors come in two main types - probe type and cable type. Probe type sensors consist of a single rod or probe inserted directly into the liquid, providing accurate measurements of the level. On the other hand, cable type sensors use a cable or rope that extends into the liquid, providing continuous measurements. Both types offer reliable and precise liquid level detection, making them suitable for various industrial applications where accurate level monitoring is essential.</p></p>
