<p><p>High-nickel ternary precursor market is divided into three main types: NCM622, NCM811, and NCA. NCM811 has a higher nickel content, providing increased energy density but requiring advanced manufacturing techniques. NCA, with a nickel-cobalt-aluminum ratio, is known for its high energy density but is also more expensive to produce.
