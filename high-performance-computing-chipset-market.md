<p><p>High Performance Computing Chipset Market includes four major types of chips: Central Processing Unit (CPU), Graphics Processing Unit (GPU), Field-Programmable Gate Array (FPGA), and Application-Specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC). CPUs are general-purpose processors designed for sequential processing tasks. GPUs excel in parallel processing, making them ideal for graphics-intensive applications. FPGAs offer flexibility through reprogrammable logic, catering to niche requirements. ASICs are customized specifically for a given task, offering high performance and efficiency but lacking flexibility. Each type targets different computing needs in the market.</p></p>
