<p><p>The Horizontal Mixed Flow Pump market offers three types based on impeller design: Semi-open Impeller, Open Impeller, and Enclosed Impeller. Semi-open impeller pumps possess a partially exposed impeller that allows for easy maintenance and is ideal for handling solids and dirty fluids. Open impeller pumps have a completely visible impeller suitable for handling clean fluids and low viscosity liquids. Enclosed impeller pumps are designed for high-efficiency applications and are commonly used in industrial settings where precision and performance are required.</p></p>
