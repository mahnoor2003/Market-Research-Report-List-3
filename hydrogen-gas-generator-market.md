<p><p>Hydrogen gas generator market is segmented into two types: Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer and PEM Electrolyzer. Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer uses an alkaline solution as an electrolyte, while PEM Electrolyzer uses a proton exchange membrane. The Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer is a well-established technology with lower efficiency but lower cost compared to PEM Electrolyzer, which offers higher efficiency and compact design. Both types play a crucial role in hydrogen production for various applications such as fuel cells, refineries, and chemical processes.</p></p>
