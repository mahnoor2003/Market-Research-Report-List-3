<p><p>Immunoassay Microplate Readers come in various types based on the method of detection they use. Absorbance Detection measures the amount of light absorbed by a substance in the sample. Colorimetric Detection measures the color change in the sample. Fluorescence Detection detects the fluorescent light emitted by the sample. Luminescence Detection measures the light emitted by chemical reactions in the sample. LED Detection uses LED light for detection. Other markets may include electrochemiluminescence, chemiluminescence, and radioactive detection methods.</p></p>
