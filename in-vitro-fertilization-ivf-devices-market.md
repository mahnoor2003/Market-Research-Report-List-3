<p><p>In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF) Devices Market includes various types such as Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI), Frozen Embryo Replacement (FER), and Pre-implantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD). ICSI involves the direct injection of a single sperm into an egg to aid fertilization. FER refers to the transfer of thawed embryos into the uterus after they have been frozen. PGD is a genetic testing procedure used to identify genetic defects in embryos before implantation. These technologies play a crucial role in assisting couples struggling with infertility to conceive.</p></p>
