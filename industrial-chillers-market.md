<p><p>Industrial chillers are essential for maintaining proper temperatures in various industrial processes. There are three main types of chillers used in industrial settings - air chillers, water chillers, and evaporative condensed chillers. </p><p>Air chillers use air to remove heat from the system, water chillers use water as a cooling medium, and evaporative condensed chillers use a combination of water and air to efficiently remove heat. Each type of chiller has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the industrial process.</p></p>
