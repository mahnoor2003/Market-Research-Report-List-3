<p><strong>The Inertial Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Attitude Heading and Reference Systems (AHRS)</li><li>Inertial Navigation Systems/Inertial Navigation Units (INUs)</li><li>Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inertial Sensors Market consists of three main types - Attitude Heading and Reference Systems (AHRS), Inertial Navigation Systems/ Inertial Navigation Units (INUs), and Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs). AHRS provides accurate orientation information for aircraft, vehicles, and other applications. INUs combine accelerometers and gyroscopes to provide position and velocity data for navigation purposes. IMUs consist of accelerometers and gyroscopes to measure linear acceleration and angular rate, used in various industries like aerospace, automotive, and consumer electronics for motion sensing and control.</p></p>
