<p><p>Insulated wall panels are used in construction for their thermal insulation properties. Two common types are Polyurethane Insulation Panels and XPS Insulation Panels. Polyurethane panels are known for their high R-value, excellent thermal performance, and energy efficiency. XPS panels are made from extruded polystyrene foam and offer good moisture resistance and durability. Both types of insulated wall panels are popular choices for building construction projects where thermal insulation is a key consideration.</p></p>
