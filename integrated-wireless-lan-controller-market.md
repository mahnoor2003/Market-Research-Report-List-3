<p><p>An Integrated Wireless LAN Controller is a device that combines the functions of a traditional wireless LAN controller with the capabilities of a wireless access point. It is used to centralize the management and control of multiple access points within a network, making it easier to deploy and maintain wireless networks.</p><p>The Integrated Wireless LAN Controller Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.1% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing adoption of wireless LAN controllers in various industries, such as healthcare, education, retail, and hospitality. The demand for high-speed and reliable wireless networks is driving the market for integrated wireless LAN controllers, as they offer better performance and security compared to standalone access points.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Integrated Wireless LAN Controller Market is the adoption of cloud-managed controllers, which allow for easier management and monitoring of wireless networks remotely. This trend is gaining traction among small and medium-sized businesses that do not have the resources to manage on-premise controllers.
