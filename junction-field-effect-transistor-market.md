<p><p>N Type Junction Field Effect Transistor is a type of transistor where majority charge carriers are negative electrons. It is widely used in applications requiring high input impedance and low power consumption. On the other hand, P Type Junction Field Effect Transistors have majority charge carriers as positive holes. These transistors are commonly used in applications requiring high output impedance and low leakage current. Both types play an essential role in the electronics industry, catering to various needs of different electronic circuits.</p></p>
