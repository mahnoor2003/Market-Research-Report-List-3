<p><strong>The Kitchen Benchtop Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Press molding and casting molding are two common types of market designs for kitchen benchtops. Press molding involves the use of high pressure to mold materials such as quartz or stone into the desired shape. This process results in a durable and seamless finish that is resistant to scratches and stains. Casting molding, on the other hand, involves pouring liquid material into a mold to create the benchtop. This method allows for more intricate designs and customization options, but may be less durable than press molding.</p></p>
