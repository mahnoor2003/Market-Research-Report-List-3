<p><p>Lab automation market consists of two main types: hardware equipment and programming software. Hardware equipment includes various tools and devices such as robots, automated liquid handlers, and automated storage and retrieval systems. These physical instruments help in automating laboratory processes to increase efficiency and accuracy. On the other hand, programming software allows users to create custom automated workflows, integrate different lab instruments, and analyze data from automation systems. Both hardware equipment and programming software are essential components of a successful lab automation setup.</p></p>
