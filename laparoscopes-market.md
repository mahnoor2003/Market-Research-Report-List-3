<p><p>Laparoscopes are widely used in minimally invasive surgeries, offering two main types of scopes - rigid and flexible. Rigid laparoscopes provide a straight view of the surgical area, while flexible laparoscopes offer a wider range of motion and access to hard-to-reach areas. These two types cater to different surgical needs and preferences, with rigid laparoscopes being more commonly used for basic procedures, while flexible laparoscopes are preferred for complex surgeries requiring greater maneuverability. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of laparoscopic procedures.</p></p>
