<p><p>Low density contrast medium market includes three main types: X-ray contrast agents, MRI contrast agents, and ultrasound contrast agents. X-ray contrast agents are used in X-ray imaging to facilitate visualization of internal structures. MRI contrast agents enhance the visibility of tissues in magnetic resonance imaging scans. Ultrasound contrast agents improve the visualization of blood vessels and tissues during ultrasound scans. Each type of contrast agent plays a critical role in improving diagnostic imaging and aiding in the accurate diagnosis of various medical conditions.</p></p>
