<p><p>The low voltage AC-DC LED driver market is categorized into three main types: Buck Driver, Boost Driver, and Multi-channel Driver. Buck Drivers regulate the LED current by lowering the input voltage. Boost Drivers increase the voltage to deliver a consistent current to the LED. Multi-channel Drivers control multiple LED channels independently, allowing for customized lighting effects. Each type of driver offers unique functionalities for powering LEDs efficiently and effectively in various lighting applications.</p></p>
