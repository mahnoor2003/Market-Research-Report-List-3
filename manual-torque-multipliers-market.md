<p><p>Manual torque multipliers come in two main types: reaction bar multiplier and reaction plate multiplier. Reaction bar multipliers utilize a long bar that the user can hold onto to generate torque, while reaction plate multipliers are designed with a flat plate that can be pressed against a sturdy surface for leverage. Both types are commonly used in industrial settings for tightening or loosening bolts and fasteners where high torque levels are required.</p></p>
