<p><p>Medical silica gel is available in two main types: raw silica gel and mixing silica gel. Raw silica gel is the pure form of silica gel and is used in various medical applications due to its high absorbency and non-toxic nature. Mixing silica gel, on the other hand, is a combination of raw silica gel with other materials such as desiccants or indicators for specific applications. Both types of medical silica gel play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and safety of pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and equipment.</p></p>
