<p><p>Microwave Integrated Circuits (MIC) are categorized into two main types: Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuits (MMIC) and Hybrid Microwave Integrated Circuits (HMIC). MMICs are fabricated on a single semiconductor substrate, offering high performance and reliability. HMICs combine the advantages of MMICs with the flexibility of hybrid technologies, allowing for the integration of different technologies on a common substrate. Both types of MICs are widely used in various applications such as radar systems, satellite communication, and wireless networks.</p></p>
