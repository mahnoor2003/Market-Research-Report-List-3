<p><p>Stand-Alone Narrowband IoT Chipsets operate independently of existing cellular networks, providing a dedicated network for IoT devices. Guard Band Narrowband IoT Chipsets utilize unused frequency bands adjacent to existing cellular networks for IoT communication. In-Band Narrowband IoT Chipsets operate within the same frequency bands as existing cellular networks, allowing for efficient use of spectrum. Each type offers unique advantages and considerations in terms of network coverage, cost, and compatibility with existing infrastructure.</p></p>
