<p><ul><li>Negative Etch Resists</li><li>Thick Negative Resists</li><li>Negative Lift-Off Resists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Negative photoresist is used in various applications within the semiconductor industry. Negative etch resists are specifically designed for etching processes, where the resist material is selectively removed to expose underlying layers. Thick negative resists are used when a thicker layer of resist is needed for certain processes. Negative lift-off resists are used for creating patterns on a substrate by selectively removing the resist material after deposition. Each type of negative photoresist serves a specific purpose in the fabrication of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
