<p><ul><li>NHS-PEG-Flurescein</li><li>NHS-Flurescein Ester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NHS-Fluorescein refers to fluorescein molecules that are conjugated to N-hydroxysuccinimide (NHS), which allows for easy coupling to other molecules in bioconjugation applications. NHS-PEG-Fluorescein involves the addition of a polyethylene glycol (PEG) linker to the NHS-Fluorescein molecule, providing increased solubility and stability. On the other hand, NHS-Fluorescein Ester is a derivative of NHS-Fluorescein that contains an ester group, allowing for specific targeting and covalent attachment to biomolecules. These variations offer different functionalities and applications in the biomedical research and diagnostic industries.</p></p>
