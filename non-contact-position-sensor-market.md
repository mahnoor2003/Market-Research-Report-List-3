<p><p>Non-contact position sensors are devices used to detect the position of an object without physical contact. Linear position sensors measure the linear displacement of an object along a straight path, while rotary position sensors measure the rotational displacement of an object around a central axis. Both types use various technologies such as magnetostrictive, inductive, capacitive, and optical to sense the position accurately. These sensors are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for precise measurement and control purposes.</p></p>
