<p><strong>The Oil-air Coolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-type</li><li>Dry-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil-air coolers are used in industrial machinery to dissipate heat generated during operation. Oil-type coolers use oil as a medium to transfer heat away from the equipment, while dry-type coolers use air as the cooling medium without any oil involved. Oil-type coolers are more efficient at cooling but require maintenance and monitoring of oil levels. Dry-type coolers are simpler and easier to maintain, but may not be as effective in cooling heavy-duty machinery. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on cooling requirements.</p></p>
