<p><ul><li>Thermal Cure</li><li>UV Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PCB inks are crucial components in the manufacturing of printed circuit boards. There are two main types of PCB inks - Thermal Cure and UV Cure. Thermal Cure inks require heat to cure and form a strong bond, while UV Cure inks use ultraviolet light for curing. Thermal Cure inks are preferred for their reliability and durability, while UV Cure inks offer faster curing times and are more environmentally friendly. Both types of inks play a key role in ensuring the efficiency and functionality of printed circuit boards.</p></p>
