<p><strong>The Polycarbonate Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phosgene Type</li><li>Non-Phosgene Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polycarbonate resin market can be categorized into two main types: Phosgene type and non-phosgene type. Phosgene type polycarbonate resin is produced through phosgene process, which involves reacting bisphenol A with phosgene. On the other hand, non-phosgene type polycarbonate resin is manufactured without the use of phosgene, using alternative methods like melt transesterification. Both types offer unique properties and applications, and the choice between the two depends on factors such as cost, environmental considerations, and specific requirements of the end product.</p></p>
