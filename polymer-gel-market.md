<p><p>Polymer gel market can be classified into two main types based on the type of polymer used: ionic and non-ionic. Ionic polymer gels contain charged ions, which play a crucial role in their swelling behavior and responsiveness to external stimuli. They are often used in applications requiring specific ion interactions. On the other hand, non-ionic polymer gels do not contain any charged ions, making them less sensitive to ion concentrations and pH changes.
