<p><p>Polyurethane (PU) resins are commonly categorized into three main types based on their application: Solvent Based Polyurethane Resins, Water Based Polyurethane Resins, and Other markets. </p><p>Solvent-based polyurethane resins contain volatile organic compounds and are typically used for applications requiring a high level of chemical resistance. Water-based polyurethane resins are environmentally friendly alternatives that offer similar performance to solvent-based resins but with lower levels of VOCs. The "Other" market includes specialty polyurethane resins tailored for specific applications such as adhesives, sealants, and coatings.</p></p>
