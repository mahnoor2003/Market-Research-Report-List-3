<p><strong>The Post Etch Residue Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous</li><li>Semi-aqueous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Post etch residue cleaners are used in the semiconductor industry to remove residues left on the surface of silicon wafers after etching processes. There are two main types of post etch residue cleaners on the market: aqueous and semi-aqueous. Aqueous cleaners use water as their main solvent, making them environmentally friendly but may require longer drying times. Semi-aqueous cleaners combine water with other solvents to achieve a balance between environmental friendliness and cleaning efficiency. Both types are essential in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
