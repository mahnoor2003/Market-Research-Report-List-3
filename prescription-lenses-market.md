<p><ul><li>Single Vision Spectacle Lenses</li><li>Progressive Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prescription lenses market consists of two main types: single vision spectacle lenses and progressive lenses. Single vision lenses are used to correct nearsightedness, farsightedness, or astigmatism in individuals who need correction for only one distance. Progressive lenses, on the other hand, are used for individuals who require correction for multiple distances like reading, computer use, and distance viewing. Progressive lenses provide a seamless transition between these distances, eliminating the need for multiple pairs of glasses. Both types of lenses play an important role in providing clear vision for individuals with refractive errors.</p></p>
