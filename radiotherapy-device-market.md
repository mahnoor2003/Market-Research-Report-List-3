<p><p>A radiotherapy device is a medical machine that uses high-energy radiation beams to destroy cancer cells and shrink tumors. These devices are commonly used in cancer treatment to either cure the disease or alleviate symptoms. Radiotherapy can be delivered externally using machines like linear accelerators or internally using implants known as brachytherapy.</p><p>The radiotherapy device market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.50% during the forecast period. Factors driving the growth of the market include the increasing incidence of cancer, technological advancements in radiotherapy devices, and rising awareness about the benefits of radiotherapy in cancer treatment. Additionally, the growing demand for non-invasive cancer treatment options and the expanding healthcare infrastructure in developing countries are expected to further fuel market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the radiotherapy device market include the development of advanced radiation therapy techniques such as intensity-modulated radiation therapy (IMRT) and image-guided radiation therapy (IGRT). These technologies help in delivering precise and targeted radiation therapy, minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissues. In addition, the integration of artificial intelligence in radiotherapy planning and treatment delivery is also gaining traction, improving treatment efficiency and outcomes.</p></p>
