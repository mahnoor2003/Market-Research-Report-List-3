<p><p>Reflective photoelectric beam sensors are commonly categorized into two main types: IR reflective and LED reflective sensors. IR reflective sensors use infrared light to detect objects within a specified range, while LED reflective sensors utilize light-emitting diodes to achieve the same function. Both types of sensors are widely used in various industries for tasks such as object detection, security monitoring, and automatic door operation. The choice between IR and LED sensors typically depends on factors such as cost, range, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
