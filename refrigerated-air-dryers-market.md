<p><p>Refrigerated air dryers are essential for removing moisture from compressed air systems to prevent damage to equipment and ensure quality air output. The market is segmented into cycling and non-cycling types. Cycling dryers are more energy-efficient as they only operate when needed, while non-cycling dryers run continuously. Cycling dryers are ideal for systems with varying air demand, while non-cycling dryers are better suited for constant-demand applications. Both types offer effective moisture removal, but cycling dryers are preferred for their energy-saving capabilities.</p></p>
