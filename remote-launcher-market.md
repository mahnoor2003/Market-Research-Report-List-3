<p><ul><li>Unidirectional</li><li>Two-way</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Remote launcher market can be categorized into unidirectional and two-way types. In unidirectional market, the launcher sends commands to the device for triggering a response, such as launching a rocket. This one-way communication style is often simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, in a two-way market, the launcher can both send commands and receive feedback from the device, allowing for more complex and interactive operations. Two-way communication enables better control and monitoring of the launcher system.</p></p>
