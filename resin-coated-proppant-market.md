<p><p>Resin Coated Proppant Market Types:</p><p>1. Curable Resin-Coated Proppant: In this type, the resin coating is applied to the proppant and then cured using heat or chemicals to harden the coating. This helps to improve the strength and durability of the proppant, making it more effective in hydraulic fracturing operations.</p><p>2. Precured Resin-Coated Proppant: In this type, the resin coating is already cured before being applied to the proppant. This process ensures a consistent and uniform coating on the proppant, enhancing its performance and longevity during fracturing operations.</p></p>
