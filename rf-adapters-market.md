<p><ul><li>In-series</li><li>Between-series</li><li>T-shaped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF adapters are commonly categorized into three main types based on their configuration - In-series, Between-series, and T-shaped adapters. In-series adapters are used to connect two cables of the same series or type. Between-series adapters are used to connect two cables of different series or types. T-shaped adapters have one input and two outputs, allowing for signal branching. Each type serves a specific purpose in RF communication systems, providing flexibility and compatibility in connecting different types of cables and devices.</p></p>
