<p><strong>The RF Mixer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Mixers</li><li>Passive Mixers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF Mixer market can be classified into two main types: active mixers and passive mixers. Active mixers are used for high frequency applications and require external power sources to operate. They offer higher conversion gain and linearity compared to passive mixers. On the other hand, passive mixers do not require external power sources and are more suitable for low frequency applications. They are known for their simple design and cost-effectiveness. Both types play a crucial role in RF systems by combining multiple signals to create a single output signal.</p></p>
