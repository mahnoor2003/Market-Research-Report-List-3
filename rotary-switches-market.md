<p><p>Rotary switches are classified based on the number of decks they possess. Single-deck rotary switches are the basic type with just one deck, while three-deck rotary switches have three separate decks. Four-deck rotary switches have four decks for increased functionality. Twelve-deck rotary switches are highly complex with twelve separate decks for a wide range of control options. The "Others" market includes rotary switches with more than twelve decks or unique configurations that do not fit into the other categories.</p></p>
