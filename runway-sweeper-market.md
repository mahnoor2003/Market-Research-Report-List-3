<p><strong>The Runway Sweeper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ride-on Runway Sweeper</li><li>Towed Runway Sweeper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Runway Sweeper market consists of two main types: Ride-on Runway Sweepers and Towed Runway Sweepers. Ride-on sweepers are operated by a person sitting on the equipment while cleaning the runway surfaces. They are suitable for larger areas and provide efficient cleaning. Towed sweepers, on the other hand, are attached to a vehicle that tows them along the runway, collecting debris as they move. They are often used for smaller areas or where a ride-on sweeper may not be practical.</p></p>
