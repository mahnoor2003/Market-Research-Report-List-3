<p><p>Rupture Discs are essential safety devices used in various industries to protect equipment from overpressure. Positive Arch Rupture Discs are designed to provide quick and full opening at a predetermined pressure, while Anti-arch Rupture Discs prevent the formation of an arch during burst. Flat Type Rupture Discs offer a flat surface for burst, ensuring uniform pressure relief. Other types include Reverse Buckling Rupture Discs and Scored Rupture Discs, each catering to specific application needs. These variations in design provide options for efficient pressure relief in different scenarios.</p></p>
