<p><strong>The Sample Rate Converters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Asynchronous Type</li><li>Synchronous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sample rate converters are utilized in audio equipment to convert digital signals from one sample rate to another. Asynchronous type converters operate independently of input and output frequencies, providing flexibility and high-quality conversion. On the other hand, synchronous type converters rely on a clock signal to synchronize input and output sample rates, ensuring accurate conversion. Both types cater to different market needs, with asynchronous converters offering versatility and synchronous converters providing precise synchronization, meeting the specific requirements of various audio equipment applications.</p></p>
