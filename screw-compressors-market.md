These compressors use two interlocking helical rotors to compress the gas and increase its pressure. <p><strong>The Screw Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-Injected Compressors</li><li>Oil-Free Compressors</li><li>Liquid Injection Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screw compressors are widely used in various industries due to their efficiency and reliability. There are three main types of screw compressors in the market: oil-injected compressors, oil-free compressors, and liquid injection compressors. Oil-injected compressors use oil for sealing and cooling, while oil-free compressors eliminate the need for oil, making them suitable for sensitive applications. Liquid injection compressors inject liquid refrigerant into the compression chamber to improve efficiency and performance. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements.</p></p>
