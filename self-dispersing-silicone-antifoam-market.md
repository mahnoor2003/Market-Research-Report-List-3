<p><p>Self-dispersing silicone antifoam is available in two main types: water-based and oil-based. </p><p>Water-based antifoam is more commonly used in applications where the presence of water is dominant, such as in wastewater treatment or industrial processes involving water-based solutions. It is effective at breaking down foam and preventing its formation.</p><p>On the other hand, oil-based antifoam is more suitable for applications where oil is present, such as in oil refining or petroleum processing. It is designed to inhibit foam formation in oil-based systems, providing efficient defoaming properties.</p></p>
