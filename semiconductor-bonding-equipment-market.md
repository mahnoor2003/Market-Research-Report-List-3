<p><strong>The Semiconductor Bonding Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire Bonder</li><li>Die Bonder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor bonding equipment market is dominated by two main types: wire bonder and die bonder. Wire bonder is used to connect semiconductor devices using thin wires, while die bonder is used to attach semiconductor chips onto a substrate. Wire bonders are commonly used in high-volume production environments, while die bonders are more suitable for precision assembly processes. Both types play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry by ensuring reliable and efficient connections between various components.</p></p>
