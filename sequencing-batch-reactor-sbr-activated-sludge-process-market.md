<p><p>Sequencing Batch Reactor (SBR) Activated Sludge Process involves treating wastewater in batches instead of a continuous flow. Continuous Fill Batch Reactor (CFBR) type market operates continuously with a constant flow of wastewater, while Intermittent market operates with breaks in between batches. CFBR allows for constant treatment, but may require more energy and resources. Intermittent market may have lower operational costs but can result in fluctuations in treatment efficiency. Both types have their own advantages and drawbacks in the context of wastewater treatment.</p></p>
