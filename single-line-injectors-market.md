<p><p>Single-line injectors are commonly divided into two types based on their operation: Parallel and Progressive market. Parallel injectors deliver fuel to all injectors simultaneously, ensuring equal distribution of fuel to each cylinder. On the other hand, Progressive injectors deliver fuel in a sequential manner, allowing for precise control of fuel delivery based on engine demand. Both types of injectors have their own unique advantages and are used in different types of engines based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
