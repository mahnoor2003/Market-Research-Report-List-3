<p><p>The Single-Stage Industrial Gas Regulator Market is divided into four main types based on their operating mechanism: Membrane Type, Spring Type, Piston Type, and Plug Type. Membrane type regulators use a flexible membrane to regulate gas flow, while Spring type regulators use a spring-loaded valve mechanism. Piston type regulators use a piston to control pressure, and Plug type regulators utilize a plug to adjust gas flow. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various industrial settings.</p></p>
