<p><p>Weather-Based Controllers in the smart sprinkler controller market utilize real-time weather data to adjust watering schedules and amounts accordingly. These controllers are programmed to respond to factors such as temperature, rainfall, and humidity to ensure that plants receive the right amount of water at the right time. Sensor-Based Controllers, on the other hand, use sensors to detect moisture levels in the soil and adjust watering schedules based on actual conditions in the garden. Both types of controllers help to optimize water usage and improve plant health.</p></p>
