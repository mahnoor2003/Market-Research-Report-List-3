<p><p>The snow melting cable market consists of two main types: self-regulating type and constant wattage type. Self-regulating cables adjust their heat output based on surrounding temperatures, making them energy-efficient and capable of preventing overheating. Constant wattage cables provide a consistent level of heat output regardless of temperature fluctuations, ensuring reliable snow melting performance. Both types are commonly used for applications such as preventing ice dams on roofs, melting snow on driveways and sidewalks, and maintaining safe walking surfaces during winter months.</p></p>
