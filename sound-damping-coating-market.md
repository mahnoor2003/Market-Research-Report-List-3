<p><p>Sound damping coatings can be categorized into sprayable and non-sprayable types. Sprayable coatings are applied using a spray gun, making them easy to cover large surfaces quickly and effectively. On the other hand, non-sprayable coatings come in pre-cut sheets or rolls that can be manually placed onto surfaces. These coatings are typically used in areas where spray application is not feasible or practical. Both types of coatings offer sound damping properties and are used in various industries for noise control purposes.</p></p>
