<p><p>The steam turbine-driven generator market consists of three main types: gas turbines, steam turbines, and water turbines. Gas turbines utilize gas combustion to drive the turbine, while steam turbines use steam generated from boiling water. Water turbines, on the other hand, use the kinetic energy of flowing water to rotate the turbine. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications, catering to a wide range of industries such as power generation, marine propulsion, and industrial manufacturing.</p></p>
