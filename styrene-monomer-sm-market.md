<p><strong>The Styrene Monomer (SM) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ethylbenzene Dehydrogenation Method</li><li>Ethylbenzene Oxidation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Styrene Monomer (SM) can be produced through two primary methods: Ethylbenzene Dehydrogenation and Ethylbenzene Oxidation. The Ethylbenzene Dehydrogenation method involves the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to form styrene, while the Ethylbenzene Oxidation method involves the oxidation of ethylbenzene to produce styrene. Both methods are commonly used in the market for producing SM, each having its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and environmental impact.</p></p>
