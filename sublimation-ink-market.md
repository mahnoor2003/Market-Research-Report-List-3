<p><p>Sublimation inks can be categorized into two main types: water-based dye sublimation ink and solvent dye sublimation ink. Water-based dye sublimation ink is known for its eco-friendly composition and ability to produce vibrant colors on various substrates. On the other hand, solvent dye sublimation ink is preferred for its durability and compatibility with a wider range of materials. Both types of inks offer high-quality printing results and are commonly used in dye sublimation printing applications.</p></p>
