<p><strong>The Surface Mount SMT Reflow Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Ovens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface Mount SMT Reflow Oven Market has two primary types- Convection Ovens and Vapour Phase Ovens. Convection Ovens use forced hot air to evenly heat the components on the PCB, ensuring reliable solder joints. On the other hand, Vapour Phase Ovens utilize a special liquid with a low boiling point to create a vapour blanket around the PCB, resulting in precise and controlled heating. Both types of ovens are crucial in the SMT process for achieving high-quality and consistent soldering results.</p></p>
