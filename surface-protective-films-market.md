<p><ul><li>Adhesive-free</li><li>Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface protective films are designed to protect surfaces from damage during transportation, storage, and installation. There are two main types of surface protective films: adhesive-free and adhesive. Adhesive-free films adhere to surfaces using static cling, making them easy to apply and remove without leaving residue. Adhesive films, on the other hand, use a layer of adhesive to securely attach to surfaces, providing a stronger and more long-lasting protection. Both types of films offer different benefits and are suited for various applications depending on the level of protection required.</p></p>
