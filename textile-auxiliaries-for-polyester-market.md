<p><p>Textile Auxiliaries for Polyester play a crucial role in enhancing the properties and performance of polyester fabrics. Thickener is used to increase the viscosity of dye solutions, while binding agents enhance the bonding between fibers. Cross-linking agents improve the strength and stability of polyester materials, and emulsifiers aid in the dispersion of dyes and chemicals. Fixing agents help in setting the dye onto the fabric, while dispersing agents ensure uniform color distribution. Defoamers reduce foam formation during processing. Other auxiliaries include softeners, anti-creasing agents, and anti-static agents.</p></p>
