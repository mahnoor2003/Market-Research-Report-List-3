<p><p>Thermostatic Radiator Valves (TRVs) come in two main types: Self-Operate TRVs and Electronics Valves. Self-Operate TRVs rely on the internal components to regulate the flow of hot water into the radiator based on temperature fluctuations in the room. On the other hand, Electronics Valves are equipped with sensors and electronic controls to automatically adjust the flow of hot water, providing more precise temperature control and potentially energy savings. Both types offer a convenient way to regulate heating in individual rooms.</p></p>
