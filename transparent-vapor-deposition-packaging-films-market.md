<p><p>Transparent vapor deposition packaging films are a type of high-barrier packaging material commonly used in the food and beverage industry. These films can be categorized into two main types: SiOx and AlOx. SiOx films are created by depositing silicon oxide onto a substrate, providing excellent oxygen and moisture barrier properties. AlOx films, on the other hand, are made by depositing aluminum oxide, offering similar high barrier protection. Both types of films are crucial for extending the shelf life of products and maintaining their freshness.</p></p>
