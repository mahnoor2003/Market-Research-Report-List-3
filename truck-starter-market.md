<p><strong>The Truck Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Truck starter market types include Direct Control and Electromagnetic Control. Direct Control starters use a solenoid switch to engage the starter motor directly, while Electromagnetic Control starters use a relay to control the flow of electricity to the starter motor. Direct Control starters are simpler and generally more affordable, while Electromagnetic Control starters offer more precise control and are often used in modern vehicles. Both types are widely used in trucks and other heavy-duty vehicles for starting the engine efficiently.</p></p>
