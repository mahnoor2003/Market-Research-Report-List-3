<p><p>Tunable pulsed lasers refer to lasers that can emit pulsed light with varying wavelengths, pulse widths, or pulse energies. The market types include pulse tunable dye lasers, which use organic dyes for wavelength tuning; pulse-width tunable fiber lasers, which allow for adjustment of pulse duration; and pulsed solid-state lasers with tunable wavelengths, using solid materials like crystals or glasses. Other types may include semiconductor lasers or gas lasers that offer tunability in different ways, catering to various applications and industries.</p></p>
