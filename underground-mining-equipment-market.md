<p><p>Underground mining equipment market types include longwall and room and pillar systems. Longwall mining involves the use of automated machinery to extract coal in a continuous process, resulting in high productivity and efficiency. Room and pillar mining involves the use of a series of interconnected rooms and pillars to extract coal, allowing for flexibility and selective extraction. Both types of systems have their advantages and are used in various mining operations based on factors like geology, ore body size, and production requirements.</p></p>
