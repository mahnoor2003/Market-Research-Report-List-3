<p><ul><li>Rigid Sealant</li><li>Flexible Sealant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV curable sealants are classified into two main types based on their flexibility: rigid sealants and flexible sealants. Rigid sealants are designed for applications where a strong and durable bond is needed, such as in construction or automotive industries. They provide high strength and stiffness once cured. On the other hand, flexible sealants are used in applications where movement or vibration is present, such as in electronics or aerospace industries. They maintain their elasticity even after curing, allowing for movement without cracking or breaking.</p></p>
