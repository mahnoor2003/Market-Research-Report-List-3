<p><p>The Vibrating Sample Magnetometer (VSM) market includes two main types: Automatic and Fully Automatic. Automatic VSMs require some manual intervention from the operator, such as loading samples and configuring measurements. Fully Automatic VSMs, on the other hand, have automated processes from start to finish without the need for operator intervention. Fully Automatic VSMs are often preferred for high-throughput applications or when precise and repeatable measurements are required. Both types cater to different needs and preferences within the market for magnetic characterization instruments.</p></p>
