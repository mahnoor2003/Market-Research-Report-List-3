<p><strong>The Vinyl Composition Tile (VCT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vinyl Composition Tile (VCT) is available in two main market types: Single Layer and Multilayer. Single layer VCT is made from a single layer of material, typically consisting of a blend of vinyl resins, plasticizers, fillers, and pigments. It is budget-friendly and easy to install, making it a popular choice for commercial spaces with high foot traffic. On the other hand, Multilayer VCT is constructed with multiple layers, including a wear layer for added durability. This type of VCT offers enhanced performance and design options, making it suitable for areas that require greater resilience and aesthetics.</p></p>
