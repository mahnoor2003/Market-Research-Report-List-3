<p><p>Vinyl Disposable Gloves come in two main types - Powder Free Disposable Gloves and Powder Coated Disposable Gloves. Powder Free gloves are free from cornstarch powder that is often used to make it easier to put on the gloves. They are preferred in industries where powder contamination is a concern. On the other hand, Powder Coated gloves have a light dusting of powder on the inside to make them easier to put on. Both types of gloves offer protection and are commonly used in medical, food service, and industrial settings.</p></p>
