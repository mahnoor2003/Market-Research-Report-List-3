<p><p>Wastewater screening equipment includes various types such as auger screens, bar screens, and drum screens that are used to remove debris and solids from wastewater. Auger screens use a rotating screw to lift solids out of the water, while bar screens use vertical or horizontal bars to trap solids. Drum screens have rotating drums with fine openings to filter out solids. Each type of screening equipment is designed to effectively separate and remove debris from wastewater for proper treatment and disposal.</p></p>
